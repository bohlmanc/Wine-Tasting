User is a wine educator. Their primary goal with Wine Pocket Pal is to help students/participants learn how to taste wine confidently — including picking new wines, finding tasting notes, and enjoying wine more broadly. They want the app to serve as an educational companion, not just a logging tool. When suggesting features or improvements, prioritize things that build understanding, vocabulary, and confidence in novice/intermediate tasters.
